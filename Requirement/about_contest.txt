🧱 Welcome to Brick by Brick - our second challenge on the path to Barcelona!

This round takes competitors into the world of LEGO® data, using a dataset built from publicly available Rebrickable data. Hidden within thousands of sets, parts, colors, themes, and inventories are stories waiting to be discovered.

 

Your challenge is simple: Use data visualization to uncover and communicate an interesting story, insight, trend, or relationship within the LEGO ecosystem.

:ballot_box_with_check: Challenge Requirements

Tell a Story: Every submission must answer a question, communicate an insight, or tell a story. This is not a dashboard-building exercise. It is a data storytelling challenge.
 

Ask yourself:

- What surprised me?

- What pattern did I discover?

- What should viewers remember after they leave my report?


2. Key Takeaway Requirement: Every submission must include a visible "Key Takeaway" statement on the landing page.

 

The statement should be:

- 50 words or fewer

- Written for a general audience

- Clearly summarize the most important insight from your report


Think of it as the headline of your story.

 

3. Data Source Attribution (Required): This challenge uses data sourced from the Rebrickable LEGO Database. All submissions must include a visible citation within the report. Submissions missing the data source attribution will not be eligible to win.


Required attribution: Source: Rebrickable LEGO Database (rebrickable.com)


Participants may clean, transform, enrich, or model the data as desired, but attribution to Rebrickable must remain visible in the final submission. We're grateful to the Rebrickable team for granting permission to use their data for the competition.
